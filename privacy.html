Privacy Policy for Crownscape

Last updated: 8 June 2026

Crownscape is a mobile puzzle game. This Privacy Policy explains how Crownscape handles information when you use the app.

Information we collect

Crownscape does not currently require an account and does not ask you to provide your name, email address, phone number or other personal details to play the game.

Game progress and settings, such as puzzle progress, sound settings, language settings, hint credits and local statistics, are saved on your device. This information is used only to provide the game experience and is not sent to us.

Support contact

If you contact us for support, we may receive the information you choose to include in your message, such as your email address, device type, language, screenshots, bug details or feedback. We use this information only to respond to your request and improve Crownscape.

Ads and purchases

Crownscape may include optional ads, rewarded ads, in-app purchases, remove ads, hint packs or support purchases in the future. If these features are added, they may be provided by third-party services such as app stores, payment providers or advertising partners. These third parties may process data according to their own privacy policies.

Crownscape will update this Privacy Policy before enabling any live ads, analytics or in-app purchases that materially change how data is handled.

Daily reminders and notifications

Crownscape may offer optional daily reminders. If enabled, reminders are used to notify you when a daily puzzle is available. You can turn reminders off in the app settings or through your device settings.

Children

Crownscape is intended as a general audience puzzle game. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through support contact, please contact us so we can delete it.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share personal information except where needed to provide support, comply with legal obligations, or operate app store, purchase, advertising or notification features if they are enabled in the future.

Data retention

Game progress and settings remain on your device unless you delete the app or reset the relevant data. Support emails may be retained for a reasonable period so we can respond to you and keep a record of reported issues.

Your choices

You can:

  • reset your local stats in the app
  • disable sound, music and daily reminders in settings
  • delete the app to remove locally saved game data
  • contact us to ask about support messages you have sent
