Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 June 2026

Crownscape is a mobile puzzle game. This Privacy Policy explains how Crownscape handles information when you use the app.

Information we collect

Crownscape does not currently require an account and does not ask for your name, address, phone number or other personal details just to play.

Game progress and settings, such as puzzle progress, sound settings, language settings, hint credits and local statistics, may be saved on your device. This information is used only to support gameplay and is not sent to us unless you choose to contact support.

Support contact

If you contact support, we may receive information you choose to include in your message, such as your email address, device type, screenshots, bug details or feedback.

Ads and purchases

Crownscape may include optional ads, rewarded ads or in-app purchases in the future. If those features are enabled, relevant third-party providers may process information according to their own privacy terms.

Notifications

If daily reminders are enabled in the future, Crownscape may use your device notification system to remind you when a daily puzzle is available.
