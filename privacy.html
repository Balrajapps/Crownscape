Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 June 2026

Crownscape is a mobile puzzle game. This Privacy Policy explains how Crownscape handles information when you use the app.

Information we collect

Crownscape does not currently require an account and does not ask you to provide your name, email address, phone number or other personal details to play the game.

Game progress and settings, such as puzzle progress, sound settings, music settings, language settings, hint credits and local statistics, may be saved on your device. This information is used to provide the game experience and is not sent to us unless you choose to contact support.

Support contact

If you contact us for support, we may receive the information you choose to include in your message, such as your email address, device type, operating system, selected language, screenshots, bug details or feedback.

We use this information only to respond to your request, investigate issues and improve Crownscape.

Ads, purchases and third-party providers

Crownscape may include optional ads, rewarded ads, in-app purchases, remove ads, hint packs or support purchases in the future. If these features are added, they may be provided by third-party services such as app stores, payment providers, advertising networks or related platform services.

These third-party providers may process information according to their own privacy policies and terms. Crownscape will update this Privacy Policy before enabling any live ads, analytics, purchases or third-party services that materially change how information is handled.

Daily reminders and notifications

Crownscape may offer optional daily reminders. If enabled, reminders are used to notify you when a daily puzzle is available. You can turn reminders off in the app settings or through your device settings.

Children

Crownscape is intended as a general audience puzzle game. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through support contact, please contact us so we can delete it.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share personal information except where needed to provide support, comply with legal obligations, or operate app store, purchase, advertising or notification features if they are enabled in the future.

Data retention

Game progress and settings remain on your device unless you delete the app or reset the relevant data. Support emails may be retained for a reasonable period so we can respond to you and keep a record of reported issues.

Your choices

You can:

  • reset your local stats in the app
  • disable sound, music and daily reminders in settings
  • delete the app to remove locally saved game data
  • contact us to ask about support messages you have sent

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
